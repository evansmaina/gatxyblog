Latest Samsung Phone Released,the Samsung Galaxy S20 Ultra 5G.

  • Technoloy, Lifestyle
  • 03 Comments

Hello guys, today I'll do a brief review of the latest Samsung gadget on the market, the Samsung Galaxy S20 Ultra 5G. Lets dive into the specs :

Technology : 2G,3G,4G,5G

SIM cards : Single SIM(Nano-SIM and/or eSIM) or Hybrid Dual SIM(Nano-SIM, dual stand-by)

Display : 6.99 inch Dynamic AMOLED 2X capacitive touchscreen with a resolution of 1440 x 3200 pixels( ~511 ppi density)

Platform : Android 10 OS with Samsung's custom One UI 2 built on top of it. It has an Exynos 990(7nm+) chipset for the global market

Memory : Internal 128GB 12GB RAM,256 GB 12GB RAM,512 GB 16 GB RAM Card slot - microSDXC(uses shared SIM slot)

Cameras : For the back it has a quad setup comprising of 108MP (wide), 48 MP (periscope telephoto) ,12 MP (wide angle) and 0.3 MP (depth) At the front we have a 40 MP selfie camera

Battery : Non-removable Li-Po 5000mAh battery Fast charging 45W

Colours : Cosmic Grey, Cosmic Black, Cloud White

Price : $1,177
